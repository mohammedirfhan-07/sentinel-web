About SENTINEL

Understanding the threat landscape

SENTINEL is an AI-powered analysis tool designed to detect psychological manipulation, coordinated influence operations, and disinformation campaigns in digital content. It produces structured Manipulation Intelligence Reports that identify specific techniques being used, who is being targeted, and what action readers should take.

Built for the bots.in Hackathon, SENTINEL addresses one of the most pressing challenges of the digital age: the weaponization of information to manipulate public opinion, exploit vulnerable populations, and undermine trust in institutions.

Manipulation Techniques We Detect

⚠️

Fear Appeals

Using exaggerated threats to override rational thinking and provoke panic-driven action.

⏰

False Urgency

Creating artificial time pressure to prevent careful evaluation of claims.

😡

Emotional Hijacking

Triggering outrage, disgust, or fear to bypass critical thinking.

👥

Fake Consensus

Manufacturing the appearance of widespread agreement to pressure conformity.

🌱

Astroturfing

Disguising coordinated campaigns as organic grassroots movements.

❌

Disinformation

Deliberately spreading false or misleading information to deceive.

⚔️

Identity Targeting

Exploiting in-group/out-group dynamics to divide and polarize.

🎭

Authority Spoofing

Impersonating credible sources to lend false legitimacy.

🔄

DARVO

Deny, Attack, Reverse Victim and Offender — a gaslighting technique.

🐕

Dog Whistles

Using coded language that signals specific meaning to target audiences.

📈

Manufactured Virality

Using bots, paid networks, or coordination to fake organic spread.

🤖

Synthetic Media

Deepfakes and AI-generated content designed to deceive viewers.
